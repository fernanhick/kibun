Delete Your Kibun Account

Warning: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your mood history, profile information, and AI reports will be immediately and irreversibly deleted.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and all login credentials
  • All mood entries and check-in history
  • AI-generated mood reports (weekly and monthly)
  • Your onboarding profile (age, goals, lifestyle info)
  • Push notification preferences
  • All other data associated with your account

How to delete your account

You can delete your account directly from the Kibun app:

1
Open the Kibun app on your device
2
Tap the Settings tab (bottom navigation)
3
Tap Account
4
Scroll down to the Danger Zone section
5
Tap Delete Account and confirm

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Need help?

If you are unable to access the app or need assistance with account deletion, please contact us at privacy@kibun.app and we will delete your account within 30 days.

For more information about how we handle your data, see our Privacy Policy.
