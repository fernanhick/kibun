<objective>
## Goal
Create a persisted notification preferences store, a local notification scheduling service, and wire both into onboarding and app launch — so users who enable reminders get daily check-in prompts at their chosen times.

## Purpose
Notifications are the primary driver of daily check-in habit formation. Without scheduled reminders, users forget to log — and kibun's value proposition (pattern recognition over time) collapses. This plan wires the "Enable reminders" onboarding step to actual OS-level notifications.

## Output
- `src/store/notificationPrefsStore.ts` — persisted Zustand store for slot selections + permission state
- `src/lib/notifications.ts` — scheduling service (configure, schedule, cancel)
- Modified onboarding to persist + schedule on "Enable reminders"
- Modified root layout to reschedule on every app launch
- `expo-notifications` plugin registered in `app.config.ts`
</objective>
