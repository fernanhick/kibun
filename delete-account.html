Option 1: Delete My Data (keep account)

Warning: This permanently deletes all your mood entries, AI reports, and profile information. Your account stays active but your history cannot be recovered.

What gets deleted

  • All mood entries and check-in history
  • AI-generated mood reports (weekly and monthly)
  • Your onboarding profile (age, goals, lifestyle info)
  • Notification preferences

What stays

  • Your account and login credentials

How to delete your data

1
Open the Kibun app
2
Tap Settings → Account
3
Scroll to Danger Zone
4
Tap Delete My Data and confirm

Option 2: Delete Account

Warning: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. Your account, login credentials, and all data are immediately and irreversibly deleted.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and all login credentials
  • All mood entries and check-in history
  • AI-generated mood reports (weekly and monthly)
  • Your onboarding profile (age, goals, lifestyle info)
  • All other data associated with your account

How to delete your account

1
Open the Kibun app
2
Tap Settings → Account
3
Scroll to Danger Zone
4
Tap Delete Account and confirm

Your account and all data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Need help?

If you are unable to access the app or need assistance with account deletion, please contact us at privacy@kibun.app and we will delete your account within 30 days.
